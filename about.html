Our Story

A Claresholm Company,
Through & Through

Rooted in Southern Alberta. Trusted by neighbours. Committed to craftsmanship.

Founded in Claresholm

Nordic Build was founded over a decade ago by a local craftsman who believed that Southern Alberta homeowners deserved more than just a coat of paint — they deserved a lasting investment in their property.

We've grown from a one-man operation to a trusted team of skilled painters and renovation specialists, but our roots — and our commitment — remain firmly in Claresholm and the surrounding communities of Nanton, Fort Macleod, and Lethbridge County.

Every project we take on is backed by full WCB coverage and $5M liability insurance, because protecting our clients and our crew is non-negotiable.

Our Approach

We don't believe in shortcuts. Before a single brush touches your walls, we invest time in thorough preparation — filling cracks, sanding surfaces, applying appropriate primers, and protecting your belongings and surroundings. This preparation work is what separates a paint job that lasts two years from one that lasts twelve.

Our team works closely with each homeowner to understand their vision, timeline, and budget. We communicate openly throughout the project and leave every job site clean and tidy. Our goal is for the experience to be as smooth and stress-free as the finished surface.
