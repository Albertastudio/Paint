What We Offer

Our Services

From a single room to a complete exterior, we bring the same level of care and precision to every project in Southern Alberta.

🏡

Exterior Painting

Wood Siding & Trim

Proper prep, priming, and climate-grade paints for wood siding that stand up to Alberta winters. We address all rot, cracks, and surface failures before coating.

Stucco & Hardie Board

Elastomeric coatings that flex through freeze-thaw cycles without cracking or peeling. Essential for Alberta's dramatic seasonal temperature swings.

Decks & Fences

UV-resistant stains and sealers to protect outdoor surfaces from sun, snow, and hail. We use penetrating oils and solid stains rated for Canadian climates.

Garage Doors & Metal

Rust-inhibiting primers and durable topcoats for metal surfaces, garage structures, and outbuildings. Long-lasting protection against Alberta's moisture and UV.

🖌️

Interior Painting

Full Home Repaints

Complete interior transformations — ceilings, walls, trim, and doors — handled with minimal disruption to your daily life. Furniture protection and thorough cleanup included.

Accent Walls & Features

Design-forward accent walls, colour blocking, and decorative finishes. We work with your colour vision or can recommend a palette suited to your space.

Cabinetry Refinishing

Factory-finish results on kitchen and bathroom cabinets without the cost of replacement. Sprayed with durable alkyd or conversion varnish for a hard, long-lasting surface.

Basement Development

Moisture-resistant paints and coatings designed for below-grade Alberta conditions. Proper vapour barrier compatibility and anti-mould products where needed.

🔧

Surface Preparation

Drywall Repair & Patching

Seamless repairs to holes, cracks, nail pops, and water damage. Texture matching to blend repairs invisibly with existing surfaces.

Caulking & Sealing

Comprehensive caulking of gaps, windows, trim transitions, and penetrations. Improves both appearance and energy efficiency.

Pressure Washing

Professional exterior cleaning to remove dirt, algae, mildew, and failing paint. An essential first step before any exterior painting project.

Popcorn Ceiling Removal

Safe removal and smooth skim-coat finish application for dated textured ceilings. We test for asbestos on pre-1990 homes before proceeding.

🏗️

Renovations & Commercial

Kitchen Refreshes

Paint, trim work, and cabinet refinishing to modernize kitchens on a budget — a fraction of the cost of a full renovation.

Bathroom Updates

Moisture-resistant paints and tile-adjacent coatings for bathrooms that stay fresh and mould-free through Alberta's temperature swings.

Commercial Spaces

Efficient, low-disruption painting for retail, office, and commercial properties. Evening and weekend scheduling available to minimize business downtime.

New Construction

Partnering with builders for full interior & exterior finishing on new builds. Consistent quality from drywall prime coat to final finish.
