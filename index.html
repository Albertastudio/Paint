Why Nordic Build

Built for Alberta. Built to Last.

Alberta's climate is one of the harshest in Canada. We use products and techniques specifically suited to its freeze-thaw cycles, intense UV, and temperature extremes.

🌡️
Climate-Tested Coatings

Alberta's extreme temperature swings demand specialized paints. We use only products rated for –40°C to +40°C cycles, ensuring your exterior won't peel, crack, or blister.

🏠
Local Expertise

We know Claresholm homes — from heritage wood siding to modern stucco. Our team has been painting across Southern Alberta for over a decade.

🛡️
Fully Insured & WCB

Full liability insurance and Workers' Compensation Board coverage on every project. Your home and our workers are fully protected, always.

🎨
Precision Craftsmanship

We don't rush. Proper surface prep, quality primers, and two-coat finishing produce results that last 8–12 years — not just two seasons.
